SMSBOTU - Internet Access via SMS powered by LLMs

Discover SMSBOTU, an intelligent agent that brings internet information to your phone via SMS, powered by Langchain and OpenAI. Originally created to target turkish soldiers without smartphone access during their mandatory military service, it now aids many in Turkey who don't have daily internet but are in GSM coverage areas. SMSBOTU transforms your SMS into a simple, direct line to the information you need, without distractions.
