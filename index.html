KU-TWIN

KU-TWIN is the very early steps of a long lasting KARMA Lab project that aims to build the digital twin of Koç University. The first step for the project was to acquire the photogrammetric model of the university campus located in Istanbul, which was followed by remodelling of all buildings based on the plans and the point cloud data. Later on we started ideating on what new possibilities, functions, abilities having access to this model would provide. Currently, this VR experience provides it's users unique experiences created for the Koç University Campus, such as time-travel within the history of Koç University, navigating around the campus shifting between different bodies (human, gull, grasshopher), seeing the campus with non-realistic textures and so on. But in the future it will serve as a holistic digital twin of the university.
